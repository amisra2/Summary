Is there no alternative?
           <br/>
           You really haven't addressed my main concerns.  What does a President do when public opinion is basically split, almost 50/50 down the middle.  Which sector of the public should influence his policy direction and which sector should be betrayed?  What does a President do when he needs to take action to protect the public even when the public doesn't agree with the action?  Announce to the public that due to their opinion, action will not be taken only to regret it later when civilians are harmed or take the action necessary and risk being unpopular because the safety of civilians is more important?
          </blockquote>
         </div>
