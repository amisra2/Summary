          <blockquote class="postcontent restore">
           By now, most people should realize the war in Iraq was not necessary. The excuse Bush and his regime used to start the war was that we had to disarm Iraq of Weapons of Mass Destruction. The British even said these WMD could be used against us within 45 minutes! As everyone now knows, the WMD did not exist. The excuse for war has been proven to be a fraud.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           What made it possible for the Bush regime and the forces for war to bring off their deadly and destructive goal successfully?
           <br/>
           <br/>
           An article in The Guardian, Thursday July 17, 2003, sheds some important light on one element that lead to the unnecessary carnage and war that is still going on despite Bush's "Mission Accomplished" show. That element is Israel.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Senior Bush regime officials, including defense secretary Donald Rumsfeld, took a bunch of neocons and formed them into a group called Office of Special Plans. The purpose of OSP was to cook the intelligence reports from the CIA and the Defense Intelligence Agency until it looked the way the forces for war wanted it to look. And the forces for war wanted to paint the false picture of Saddam and Iraq as a threat to the US and the "free" world.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The Guardian article reported:  "The OSP was an open and largely unfiltered conduit to the White House not only for the Iraqi opposition. It also forged close ties to a parallel, ad hoc intelligence operation inside Ariel Sharon's office in Israel specifically to bypass Mossad and provide the Bush administration with more alarmist reports on Saddam's Iraq than Mossad was prepared to authorize.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "'None of the Israelis who came were cleared into the Pentagon through normal channels,' said one source familiar with the visits. Instead, they were waved in on Mr. Feith's authority without having to fill in the usual forms.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "The exchange of information continued a long-standing relationship Mr. Feith and other Washington neo-conservatives had with Israel's Likud party.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "In 1996, he and Richard Perle - now an influential Pentagon figure - served as advisers to the then Likud leader, Binyamin Netanyahu. In a policy paper they wrote, entitled A Clean Break: A New Strategy for Securing the Realm, the two advisers said that Saddam would have to be destroyed, and Syria, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, and Iran would have to be overthrown or destabilized, for Israel to be truly safe."
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The reason Americans are now dying and being maimed in Iraq is to make sure Israel is "truly safe." The pro-Israel neocons can't come right out and say Americans must die to ensure Israel's safety. Instead they get a greedy, ignorant, egotistical individual like Bush, have one of their own (Paul Wolfowitz) whisper in his ear to destroy Saddam, and the rest is history/current event!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Americans should prepare themselves to sacrifice more for the chosen people! After all, Iraq was just one nation the neocons and the Jews want regime change in. We will have to spend more priceless lives and tens of billions of dollars more in Syria, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, and Iran!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Already the main stream media is parroting the lie from Israel that perhaps Iraq hid the nonexistent WMD in Syria. The Guardian article reported that intelligence sources said that lie originated in the office of the Israeli prime minister.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           When will Americans, especially those in the US armed forces, say enough is enough? After Syria? After Iran? Or, hopefully, NOW!
