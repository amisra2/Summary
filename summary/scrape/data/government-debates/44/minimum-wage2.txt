Therefore, what minimum wage esablishes is how much the companies pay X. And that money, in turn, comes out of the profits paid to the executive. It won't come out of firing more workers, because then the company would fall below X and be unable to operate, and repealing min. wage would not cause more hirings, because the company has no reason to hire more than X.
