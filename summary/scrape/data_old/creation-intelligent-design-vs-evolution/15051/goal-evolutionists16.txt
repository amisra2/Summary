           Ok, "Freedom's view point on evolution 101":
           <br/>
           <br/>
           1. I do not believe the mechanism of descent with selective modification explains the advancement of life. When I see that mechanism used in an attempt to explain advancement I call it irrational evolution, when I say rational evolution (or just evolution) I mean the actual result of that mechanism... which certainly allows for adaptation and refinement. I do not believe any experiment or evidence ever discovered or performed has demonstrated the proposed mechanism of advancement, nor even does that mechanism work "on paper".
           <br/>

           2. I reject the dichotomy between irrational evolution and creationism.
