           <i>
            aren't
           </i>

               One occurrence is even associated with dinosaur footprints on the same surface, on top of a coal seam
              </b>

A dinosaur's footprint, hardening beside a tree that supposedly grew to full size in a few hours under thousands of feet of sludgy water (and yes, it would have to be thousands upon thousands of feet of water for it to hold enough material to deposit the formation).
