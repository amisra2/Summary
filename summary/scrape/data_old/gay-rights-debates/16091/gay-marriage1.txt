               <br/>
               <br/>

               Tax breaks are not a shallow goal to pursue, they are most noble; but there is nothing special about homosexuals which are making them endure taxation. At the heart of the issue there is no discrimination, only anti-discrimination. Homosexual advocates are complaining about not being given the same positive discrimination heterosexuals have. The indifference of the government and the general public towards homosexual marriage (A and B) is being demonized as an attack on A and B. A violation of rights.
