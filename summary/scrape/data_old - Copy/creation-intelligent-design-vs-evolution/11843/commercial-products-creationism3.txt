            suggest
           </i>
           either one.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Saying that our understanding of the common descent of all life has had no commercial application strikes me as akin to saying that our understanding of how birds manage to fly has had no commercial application
           <i>
            because we don't make birds.
           </i>
           It's a little obtuse, yes? XXX-backward, even. The study of how birds flew led directly to our understanding of aerodynamics and all derivative commercial applications. Similarly, study of the compelling evidence for common descent led to the development of the theory of evolution and all
           <i>
            its
           </i>
           derivative commercial applications.
          </blockquote>
         </div>
