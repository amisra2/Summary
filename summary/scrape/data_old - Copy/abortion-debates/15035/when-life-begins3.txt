Every time a contraceptive is used, sperm are being killed (as well as any other time sperm is otherwise 'wasted.')... potential humans are being killed. That's all a zygote/embryo is--a potential human. It still needs a lot more to become a human, just like an egg needs a lot more, or a sperm needs a lot more.
